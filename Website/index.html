Prevention Strategies

Reducing the impact of chronic diseases requires proactive measures. Here are some effective strategies to minimize your risk:

  • Adopt a balanced diet and stay physically active.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Monitor and manage existing health conditions.
