Risk Factors & Prevention Strategies

Chronic diseases such as diabetes, asthma, and heart conditions are influenced by several modifiable risk factors, including:

  • Poor diet and physical inactivity
  • Smoking and alcohol consumption
  • Environmental pollutants

Implementing healthy lifestyle practices can significantly reduce these risks.
